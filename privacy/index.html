Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026 · MCL Electric LLC, Lincolnwood, IL

The short version

We collect only what you choose to send us, we use it only to respond to you and do the work you ask for, and we never sell it. This site sets no cookies of its own and runs no advertising trackers.

Information we collect

  • Contact form submissions: name, phone number, email address, town, and the message you write. You provide this voluntarily when requesting service or a quote.
  • Phone, text, and email: if you contact us directly, we keep the correspondence needed to schedule and complete your work.

How it's used

  • To respond to your inquiry, schedule service, prepare estimates, and perform electrical work you request.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing.

Third-party services this site uses

  • FormSubmit.co relays contact-form submissions to our email inbox. Your form data passes through their service to reach us.
  • GitHub Pages hosts this website and, like any web host, may keep standard server logs (such as IP address and pages visited).
  • Google Fonts supplies the typefaces on this site; loading them sends your IP address to Google.

We do not currently run analytics, advertising pixels, or tracking cookies on this site.

Retention & your choices

  • We keep inquiry and job correspondence as long as needed for business and legal record-keeping.
  • You can ask us to delete your contact information at any time by emailing info@mclelectric.org — we'll honor it unless a legal or warranty obligation requires keeping a record.
  • Prefer not to use the form? Call us at (872) 378-0533.

Children

This site is for a commercial electrical contracting business and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes & contact

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and its effective date. Questions: info@mclelectric.org or (872) 378-0533.
