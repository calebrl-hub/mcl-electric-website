Healthcare electrical, done to healthcare code

Medical spaces are governed by their own NEC article — patient-care vicinity rules, redundant grounding, dedicated equipment circuits, isolated grounds for sensitive diagnostics. It's not general commercial work with exam tables; it's a specialty. MCL has built it and passed it.

What we handle

  • Complete clinic & urgent-care build-outs
  • X-ray & imaging equipment connections
  • Dedicated circuits for medical & dental equipment
  • Exam-room and procedure lighting
  • Patient-care-area code compliance (NEC 517)
  • Low-voltage rough-ins — data, nurse call, access
  • Tenant improvements in occupied medical buildings
  • Fast service response — practices can't run dark

Real healthcare work

  • American Family Care, Cicero — complete urgent-care build-out incl. X-ray connections (case study)
