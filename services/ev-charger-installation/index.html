Level 2 charging, done properly

An EV charger is one of the largest single loads in a modern home — installing one is not a plug-swap. We start with a real load calculation on your panel, tell you honestly whether your service can handle it (and what it costs if it can't), then run a dedicated circuit that meets code and keeps the charger — and everything else in the house — running safely.

What's included

  • Panel load assessment before you buy anything
  • Dedicated 240V circuits, sized to your charger
  • Tesla Wall Connector, ChargePoint, Emporia & all major brands
  • Hardwired or NEMA 14-50 outlet installs
  • Panel & service upgrades when needed
  • Multi-unit and workplace charging for buildings & businesses
  • Permits and inspection coordination
  • Clean conduit runs — garages, exterior walls, parking areas

Owners of condos and multifamily buildings: we've done building-wide electrical across Chicago — from a full building rehab in Lincoln Park to tenant build-outs downtown — so shared-panel and common-area charging questions don't scare us.
